~ Our Projects ~

Our team of experts is dedicatedly working on projects related to Lal Shahbaz Qalandar. Our aim is to spread the accurate knowledge about Qalandar's life, works, and his devotion to Allah, which reflects our profound love and respect for him.

Documentary

By visuals and interviews, we will explore and present the true legacy of Lal Shahbaz Qalandar into the mystical world of Sufi devotion and his practical vision of life.

Library

Our experts have collected extensive collection of books, and other materials related to Qalandar's life and work.

Mastam Studio

"Mastam Studio" is a production house that specializes in creating high-quality content about Lal Shahbaz Qalandar,s original poetry and it's multilingual translations.

Revival of Art & Culture

Through cultural practices , depicting in arts and literature we aim to promote a deeper understanding of Qalandar's message among people and it's profound and positive impact on society.Hence it's main focus is revival and Preservation through documentation of our Regional and local Culture with it's Literature and language.

University

Our university will strive to foster a community that promotes true message of Love ,peace and Humanity through learning about Qalandar and spreading positivity about them to the world.It will also educate the inhabitants of Sehwan to cope with contemporary world challenges in the field of Science, humanities, Language and Literature, psychology, Sociology, history, anthropology and Fine art.

Research Center

Through extensive research and analysis ,we aim to shed light on Research gapes and, false and unauthentic data which is creating disinformation about biography, poetry ,mission and teachings of Lal Shahbaz Qalandar.Most prominently false practices which have brought stereotypes and defamed image of the shrine.
